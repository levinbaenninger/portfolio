# Meetique

Meetique is a project designed to help users meet with an agent, receive a clear summary, and stay focused during their interactions. The main goal is to streamline agent meetings and provide concise summaries for better productivity.

## Problem Statement

In today's fast-paced world, meetings with agents can often be unproductive and lack clarity. Users may struggle to stay focused during these interactions, leading to missed information and inefficient communication. There is a need for a solution that can help users stay on track, receive clear summaries, and enhance their overall meeting experience.

## Solution

Meetique addresses these challenges by providing a platform that facilitates effective meetings with agents. The key features of Meetique include:

- **Meeting Facilitation**: Meetique offers tools to help users prepare for meetings, set agendas, and stay focused during interactions with agents.
- **Clear Summaries**: After each meeting, Meetique generates concise summaries that highlight key points and action items, ensuring users have a clear understanding of the discussion.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: The platform is designed with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to navigate and utilize its features effectively.
